About Me

Hi, I'm Tony. Welcome to my personal website! As of right now, I'm a high school sophomore living in California. I thought it would be a good idea to make my own website and practice my coding skills and have a reference for what I've been doing so far in my free time, so here it is! This website was created using minimal HTML and CSS; it's published and supported by GitHub Pages for free! You can use the navigation bar above to navigate through my website and learn more about me! Please note that this website is best viewed on a laptop or desktop using a modern browser, I have yet to add responsive design to this and will try to soon!

Currently, I don't have the greatest idea of what I'd like to do in the future, but I'm leaning towards options like engineering, computer science, and really anything STEM-related!

Contact Information

You can contact me through my personal email for any reasons, I can typically respond within a day if I'm at home, which I usually am since the pandemic is still happening as of right now.

Other Online Profiles:

  • My Github profile - some small programming projects and the code for this website if you'd want to see it
  • My Lichess profile - typically active on here each day; currently ~2000 bullet/blitz, ~2400 rapid, ~2500 puzzles
  • My Chess.com profile - not very active on here, I prefer and mainly use lichess over chess.com

Some Things I'm Currently Doing

Robotics For All

Currently a Lead Instructor at Robotics For All, a non-profit, volunteer-run organization dedicated to providing accessible STEM education for low-income families. I've taught programming classes online through Zoom to elementary school students; in topics like code.org, HTML, and CSS. I've also recently earned the Gold Level of the President's Volunteer Service Award through RFA, with 200+ hours of community service from various organizations in the past year.

Piano

Playing and practicing since 5 years old, planning to take the California Certificate of Merit Level 10 test in Spring 2021. I've learnged mostly classical pieces, from artists like Bach, Mozart, Chopin, Schubert, and Clementi, to name a few. Videos of me performing my testing pieces coming soon!

Chess Club

Club secretary of my high school's chess club - I give lectures during meetings, and handle most of the club communication(emails, Discord, iMessages). We've connected with other high school chess clubs in the Bay Area to hold an inter-school chess tournament earlier this year and have been planning another one. We also have been reaching out to local elementary schools and libraries, and have been teaching chess weekly to elementary school students online during the pandemic.

Competitive Programming

Recently I've been learning about competitive programmming, and participating in the USA Coding Olympiad(USACO) contests. I'm currently still hardstuck in the Bronze division, but I've been practicing in my spare time and have been learning about programming, computer science, and algorithms. My current plan is to try to get to Silver by the April 2021 competition, study up over the summer, and hopefully reach Gold sometime in the 2021-2022 season!

Some Things I've Done

Chess

Played competitively since 6th Grade, competed in over 120 tournaments rated by the United States Chess Federation(USCF) mostly throughout the Bay Area. By spending most of my weekends since sixth grade playing in chess tournaments, it’s helped me improving my critical thinking abilities tremendously. Since chess is a mind sport, it’s also helped me develop valuable problem solving and planning skills, which are great life skills I’ve put to use at school, home, and anywhere in between on a daily basis! I've been playing in online tournaments recently, which sadly are nowhere near the quality of over-the-board tournaments, and I practice daily on chess sites like lichess.

  • My USChess Member Page
    • 2nd Category Title
    • Three 1st Category Norms
    • Peak Standard Rating: 1816

Notable Achievements(not very impressive):

  • 2018 CalChess Grade 8 State Champion
  • 2018 California Class Warfare Championship AB Section 1st Place
  • 2019 CalChess State Championship A Section Tied 2nd Place
  • 2019 Bay Area Chess Winter Championship AB Section Tied 1st Place

BlueStamp Engineering

  • My BlueStamp 2020 Project Website

Engineering summer program I did summer of Freshman year online. In three weeks, I was able to create a Fingerprint ID Safe with a Keypad over the course of 3 weeks, operated by an Arduino, arduino code, circuitry, a keypad, a fingerprint scanner, and hot glue. I was able to learn a lot about Arduino circuitry, coding, and the basics of engineering, during the global pandemic. It was a great experience to learn and have practice with engineering, and I'd highly recommend it for any high schoolers interested.

White Stag Monterey

Volunteered and helped to plan out and lead 10-12 year olds at a leadership-themed, outdoor summer camp at White Stag Monterey; met for a weekend each month during the school year to plan out two weeks of a summer camp in Piney Creek, CA. This was a great experience I did during my freshman year which helped me get hands-on leadership experience and I had tons of fun while doing so!

Some of My Hobbies

Cooking

Since quarantine, I've picked up cooking as a hobby. I still remember back in April, when I was browsing YouTube, I came upon a video by J. Kenji Lopez-Alt, where he made a French omelette in under two minutes! I was then inspired to try making one myself, and during my first attempt, I remember failing and making scrambled eggs instead. Since then, I've improved my cooking skills vastly. Thanks to the help of YouTube, I've been able to learn how to cook so I won't have to rely solely on instant ramen when I go to college! My favorite dishes to cook are pastas(bolognese and pomodoro are my favorites), steak, and breakfast burritos/tacos! I've been able to(decently) cook more dishes such as oyakodon, tonkatsu, chicken katsu, burgers, Japanese curry, omelettes, fried rice, matcha lattes, avocado toast, and much more! I's been a valuable life skill that I've been able to develop and will cherish for the rest of my life! Here are some YouTubers I watch and look for inspiration in when cooking either for myself or my family:

  • J. Kenji Lopez-Alt
  • Binging With Babish
  • Adam Ragusea

Pictures coming soon!

Running

Also since quarantine, partially as a result of the hobby above and the staleness of everything being online, I've also been gaining some weight. To counteract this, I've gotten into exercising on the treadmill! As of right now, I try to run 2 miles, first half a mile at 3mph, and the rest at 5mph, with water breaks every half mile. I typically try to run every 1-2 days, or when my schedule permits, and it's something I'm hoping to continue throughout my life to stay fit!

The Stock Market

Also since a few months ago(January 2021), I've been interested in the stock market as well, mostly because of what's been happening with GameStop. In my free time, it's been very fun and worthwhile to learn about financial terms and processes, investing, and the stock market in general! I've also begun doing my own research on stocks and investing some of my savings as well.
