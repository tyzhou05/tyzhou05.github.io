Cooking

Since quarantine, I've picked up cooking as a hobby. Thanks to the help of YouTube, I've been able to learn how to cook so I won't have to rely on instant ramen when I go to college! I've been able to(decently) cook dishes such as oyakodon, tonkatsu, pasta carbonara and bolognese, steak, burgers, omelettes, breakfast tacos, fried rice, matcha lattes, avocado toast, and much more! Here are some YouTubers I watch and look for inspiration in when cooking either for myself or my family:

  • J. Kenji Lopez-Alt
  • Binging With Babish
  • Adam Ragusea
