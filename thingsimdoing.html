Some Things I'm Currently Doing

Robotics For All

Currently a Lead Instructor at Robotics For All, a non-profit, volunteer-run organization dedicated to providing accessible STEM education for low-income families. I've taught programming classes online through Zoom to elementary school students; in topics like code.org, HTML, and CSS. I've also recently earned the Gold Level of the President's Volunteer Service Award through RFA, with 200+ hours of community service from various organizations in the past year.

Chess Club

Club secretary of my high school's chess club - I send emails, give lectures during meetings, and handle most of the club communication. We've connected with other high school chess clubs in the Bay Area to hold an inter-school chess tournament earlier this year and have been planning another one. We also have been reaching out to local elementary schools and have been teaching chess weekly to elementary school students online.

Competitive Programming

Recently I've been learning about competitive programmming, and participating in the USA Coding Olympiad(USACO) contests. I'm currently still hardstuck in the Bronze division, but I've been practicing in my spare time and have been learning about programming, computer science, and algorithms!
