Some Things I'm Currently Doing

Robotics For All

Currently a Lead Instructor at Robotics For All, a non-profit, volunteer-run organization dedicated to providing accessible STEM education for low-income families. I've taught programming classes online through Zoom to elementary school students; in topics like code.org, HTML, and CSS. I coordinate with teachers' assistants, write and send emails, and am in charge of classes.

I'm also currently helping out with recruitment-related tasks, curriculum development, website development, and more miscellaneous tasks. I've also been awarded the Gold Level of the President's Volunteer Service Award through RFA, with 200+ hours of community service from various organizations in the past year. I have also recently been recognized as one of their Volunteers of the Month!

Piano

Playing and practicing since 5 years old, planning to take the California Certificate of Merit Level 10 test in Spring 2022. I've learned mostly classical pieces, from artists like Bach, Mozart, Chopin, Schubert, and Clementi, to name a few. Videos of me performing coming soon!

Chess Club

Club co-vice president of my high school's chess club - I give lectures during meetings, and handle most of the club communication(emails, Discord, iMessages). I've helped to connect with other high schools in the Bay Area to hold multiple inter-school chess tournaments. We also have been reaching out to local elementary schools and libraries, and have been teaching chess weekly to elementary school students online during the pandemic. We've also been recently recognized with a Community Service Club Award at our high school for our teaching efforts!

Competitive Programming

Recently I've been learning about competitive programmming, and participating in the USA Coding Olympiad(USACO) contests. I'm currently still hardstuck in the Bronze division, but I've been practicing in my spare time and have been learning about programming, computer science, and algorithms.
