Some Things I've Done

Chess

Played competitively since 6th Grade, competed in over 120 tournaments rated by the United States Chess Federation(USCF) mostly throughout the Bay Area. By spending most of my weekends since sixth grade playing in chess tournaments, it’s helped me improving my critical thinking abilities tremendously. Since chess is a mind sport, it’s also helped me develop valuable problem solving and planning skills, which are great life skills I’ve put to use at school, home, and anywhere in between on a daily basis! I've been playing in online tournaments recently, which sadly are nowhere near the quality of over-the-board tournaments, and practice daily on sites like lichess.
